Tools

For starters, you need these necessary part in order to start you first Gundam build. Of course, you gonna need your Gundam kit. But the second picture above are the most important tools you need.

From your left to right in order:
  • Plastics Hobby Nippers
  • Hobby Knife
  • Sanding Sticks/Sponge
  • Gundam Marker for Pen Lining

Gundam Content

Now let's open the box! Most cases, there are 3 important parts inside the kit box:
  • Runners that hold the building parts
  • Manual for following the building instructions
  • Decals.
However, in this build there is only 2 contents.

Cut the Right Way

In this picture above, the plastic that is holding the part of the Gundam is called the gate. You better cut those gates with your plastic hobby nippers. As you can see in the picture, I am cutting a little bit away from the part that is because to avoid any damage to part. It will have hole or even accidentally cut your part.
